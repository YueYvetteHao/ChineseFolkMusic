Four 陕北 (Northern Shaanxi) tunes — 三十里铺, 山丹丹开花红艳艳, 兰花花, 脚夫调 — against four 江南 (Jiangnan) tunes — 茉莉花, 紫竹调, 无锡景, 牧童短笛. For the three pieces with two themes (山丹丹开花红艳艳, 紫竹调, 无锡景), the themes are combined into one sample per tune. Every consecutive interval is measured in semitones; the panels below aggregate and test the two sets. (Interval stats are pitch-only.)

Per-tune statistics

mean |leap| = mean absolute interval (semitones); % ≥ P4 = share of intervals a perfect 4th or wider (≥ 5 st); % ≤ M3 = share that are 3rds or smaller steps (1–4 st); osc = chained direction-reversing wide leaps. Remainder to 100% = unisons (repeated notes).

Aggregate interval distribution (陕北 vs 江南)

Each bar = that interval's share of the region's intervals. The crossover is the whole story: 江南 peaks on 3rds (m3/M3), 陕北 peaks on 4ths/5ths (P4/P5).

Wide-leap share by tune

Mean leap vs wide-leap %

Each point is one tune. The clusters separate on the vertical axis (how often wide leaps occur), far more than on the horizontal (average leap size).

Oscillating leaps — the 陕北 saw-tooth

A leap up answered by a leap down (both ≥ P4). This chained, direction-reversing motion is the 陕北 signature and is almost absent in 江南.
