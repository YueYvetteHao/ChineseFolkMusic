沂蒙山小调 Yimeng Mountain Tune Shandong 山东

A Shandong (山东) folk song that grew from a 1940 tune (lyrics 阮若珊 / Ruan Ruoshan, music 李林 / Li Lin) into a lyrical ode to the Yimeng (沂蒙) hills; famous for its "fish-biting-tail" (鱼咬尾) phrase-chaining. Included as a third-texture extension beyond the Shannxi-vs-Jiangnan contrast.  ·  Standalone example — melody embedded; opens by double-click, no server.
