贺绿汀 (He Lüting)'s 1934 piano piece — folk-idiom pentatonic, not a collected song — which won Alexander Tcherepnin's prize for "a piano piece in Chinese style" and became a landmark of Chinese piano music. Included as a folk-inspired melody; the A-section pastoral theme is used here.  ·  Standalone example — melody embedded; opens by double-click, no server.
