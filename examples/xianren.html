A Xi'an-dialect song (2016) by 程渤智 (Cheng Bozhi) that became a city anthem, packed with Xi'an landmarks and local color. Its syllabic, speech-like delivery (~30% repeated pitches, narrow motion, few leaps) makes it a "speech-song bridge" near the speech end of the speech→song continuum.  ·  Standalone example — melody embedded; opens by double-click, no server.
